Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

1. Introduction

DreamVersAI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo (if provided)

2.2 Usage Data

  • Generated images and videos (stored temporarily)
  • Prompts and generation settings
  • App usage statistics
  • Device information (model, OS version)

2.3 Payment Information

  • Subscription status (processed by Apple/Google)
  • Transaction history
  • Note: We do NOT store credit card details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Process your subscription
  • Improve our AI models and services
  • Send important service updates
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location: Your data is stored on secure servers using Firebase and Google Cloud.

4.2 Security Measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

4.3 Retention: Generated content is stored for 30 days, then automatically deleted unless saved to your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google), RevenueCat, AI API providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party AI services:

  • OpenAI (DALL-E)
  • Replicate (Flux, Stable Diffusion)
  • KLING AI (Video generation)
  • CivitAI (Custom models)

These services may process your prompts and generated content according to their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and marketing
  • Data Portability: Export your data

8. Children's Privacy

DreamVersAI is NOT intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover underage usage, we will immediately delete the account.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Essential Cookies: For authentication and security
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics (can be disabled)
  • No Advertising Trackers: We do not use ad networks

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located in the United States and Europe. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Google Cloud's data protection agreements
  • EU-US Data Privacy Framework compliance

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or in-app notification.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@dreamversai.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@dreamversai.com

13. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Right to restrict processing

14. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of sale (Note: We do NOT sell data)
